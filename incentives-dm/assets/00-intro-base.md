# Decision-making under Uncertainty using Visualizations

Thank you for choosing to participate!

You will review 18 scenarios (trials) in total (excluding attention checks). Before you work on the actual trials, you will learn about the graphs and practice with your task. You can also review the graphs and your task again before each block. Please read these instructions carefully as your task changes for each block.

The study should take about 8 minutes, and you will receive $3.5 for your participation. Your decisions will be scored as correct or incorrect.

You can quit at any point, but be aware that you cannot resume once this window is closed, and nor will you be compensated. After you have finished all the trials, you will be redirected back to Prolific.
