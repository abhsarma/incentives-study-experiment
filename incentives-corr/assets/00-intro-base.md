# Study on Correlation Judgements using Visualizations

Thank you for choosing to participate!

In this study, you will be shown pairs of charts and you will have to identify the charts with the higher correlation. The study consists of 40 trials, excluding any attention checks. Before you work on the actual trials, you will learn about the graphs, and practice (and receive feedback) on your task. You can also review the graphs and your task again using the *help* button on the top right corner of the page. Please read these instructions carefully.

The study should take about 10 minutes, and you will receive $3.5 for your participation. Your decisions will be scored as correct or incorrect.

You can quit at any point, but be aware that you cannot resume once this window is closed, and nor will you be compensated. After you have finished all the trials, you will be redirected back to Prolific.
